Restaurant

Our restaurant EL GUERIDÓN offers, in a modern, functional space, a large decorative window which floods the room with light making it bright during the day and welcoming and relaxing at night. Regardless of whether the customer is staying in the hotel they can enjoy our Chef´s menu which includes top quality ingredients with a modern twist. Our cuisine includes fresh, seasonal produce: meat and grilled fish, a large variety of vegetable dishes and delicious meals that seek to offer a harmony of flavours, aromas and textures.
