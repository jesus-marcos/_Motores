Privacy Policy


IDENTITY AND ADDRESS OF ENTITY RESPONSABLE FOR THE FILE WHERE YOUR DATA IS FOUND

Hotel Ciudad de Móstoles inform you that, upon entering your data through electronic forms on our website or upon sending your “curriculum vitae”, you shall provide us with certain Personal Data that shall be automated and incorporated into the corresponding mixed files previously registered before the Spanish Data Protection Agency of which the entity responsible for the file is or shall be the holder and responsible entity and, in your case, in charge of its processing.

PURPOSE OF PROCESSING YOUR DATA

The purpose of collecting and processing your Personal Data in an automated fashion is the following: Customer management for services and lodging at our hotels. If you send us your “Curriculum Vitae”, the purpose shall be to study “curricula” so that, through our Human Resources department, selection can be carried out of personnel that meet a suitable profile to be hired by the company. You consent that your data be processed this way and not be used for any other purpose.

Likewise, you hereby consent that your data be processed for promotional and marketing purposes, including sending electronic commercial communications for the products and services we offer, as well as others from the hotel, travel, cuisine and leisure sector. On the other hand, if you make a reservation at any of our member hotels, hotel ciudad de Móstoles shall be in charge of the processing and the member Hotel where you stay shall be responsible for your data.

TRANSFER

We inform you that in compliance with current legislation your data are transferred to State Security Forces and Corps. Due to the particular characteristics of Hotel ciudad de Móstoles activity and services and to optimally develop and achieve its objectives, transfer of personal data to third parties, including international transferences, is necessary. Likewise, you hereby consent that the personal data you submit to us, including default of payment, be transferred to hotel ciudad de Móstoles Group companies and associates for the purposes mentioned, particularly for promotional and marketing ends, not only of Hotel Ciudad de Móstoles but of associates. All Hotel Ciudad de Móstoles company and associates are referenced on our www.hotelciudaddemostoles.com website.

When you send us your CV, the entity in charge of the file will inform you that your personal data shall be transferred to hotel ciudad de Móstoles company and associates of the hotel sector referenced in the Legal Notice, so that you can participate in the selection processes of the Group’s companies and its associates.

USE OF COOKIES

Likewise, we inform you that when you connect to our website, the computer where we have it housed automatically recognizes your computer’s IP address, the day and time you entered, the time you left, and which parts of our website you visited. It is necessary for our computer to know this to be able to communicate with your computer in order to send what you request through your browser and see it on your screen. Neither we nor our computer will know your personal data if you do not provide it to us.

The computer where the website is lodged uses “cookies” to improve the service we provide. These “cookies” install themselves automatically, but do not contain any information about you, just technical information that makes it possible to better communicate with our computer. This information shall only be processed globally and statistically.

The entity in charge of the file has adopted the legally required measures of security in its facilities, systems and files according to what is stipulated in RD. 1720/2007 of 21st of December through which the Regulation to implement Organic Law 15/1999 is approved, based on the sensitivity of the data you provide.
