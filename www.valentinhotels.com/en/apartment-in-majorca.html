Rooms

The hotel has in its main building a total of 149 rooms distributed over five floors. Only a few meters away there are 52 apartments, which are divided into three buildings on four floors, each with an elevator.

The apartments are separated into a bedroom and a living room with a sofa couch, a kitchenette and a fully equipped bath room.
The hotel rooms dispose of a fully equipped bath room.

In addition the rooms and apartments have the following amenities:

  • Satellite TV
  • Direct dial telephone
  • Hair dryer
  • Safety deposit box with charge
  • Central Air condition / heating
  • Balcony
