Touristic Groups


At Valentín Hotels we count on a number of hotels specialised in touristic groups.

We also organize airport transfers and excursions in the various destinations.
You will have an individual programme for each group adjusted to their needs as well as personalized assistance
by a member of Valentín Hotels.

Actual offers:

Mallorca

In fall, winter and spring we offer you several group tours with different mottoes in Majorca. Get to know the island´s nature and culture in a new way and let the island surprise you!

Stay a week at our hotel and get to know the "green" Majorca!
