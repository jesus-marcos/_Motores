Valentin Sancti Petri Spa & Convention Centre ****

The Hotel Valentín Sancti Petri Spa & Convention Centre is situated in the well known tourist area of Novo Sancti Petri, very close the village of Chiclana de la Frontera and directly beside the wide beach Playa La Barrosa. This 4-star hotel, opened 2002, is for many reasons, suitable for common beach holidays as well as business travellers.

Crossing through some dunes, the marvellous beach is only a few metres away from the hotel: you will discover a beach without any obstruction which invites to long walks.

The Valentín Sancti Petri Spa & Convention Centre nestles in a wonderful and generously created green area of over 25.000 m2. On each side of the Valentín Sancti Petri Spa & Convention Centre there is one swimming pool especially created to fulfil the different needs of our guests. On one side you will find an area suitable for families due to its pool which is also suitable for children, the kiddie’s pool and the children’s playground. Right next to it our bar offers you different snacks and beverages. In the other part of the hotel there is a different and quieter atmosphere where you will find a second outdoor pool with a jacuzzi. Beside this second swimming pool there is our A la carte restaurant where you can enjoy a wide range of local, national and international plates. If you need more relaxation you can indulge yourself with a treatment of our professional wellness team and let you immerse in a water made oasis of well-being. Inside the hotel offers a gym, an indoor pool with jacuzzi, club thermal with sauna and turkish bath and a lot more.

More active guests can participate in a great number of sports activities (water gym, water polo, archery) organized by our animation team. In addition the hotel offers a beach volley ball field, table tennis and a games room. Children can either participate in one of our mini clubs or be taken care off in the day nursery. After the daily Mini disco, there will be entertainment with our animation team or professional shows. To end the evening there is nothing better than enjoying the pleasant temperatures and a good cocktail on our terrace.
