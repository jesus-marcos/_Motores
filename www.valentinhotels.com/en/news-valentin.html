Valentin Perla Blanca Adults Only

Valentin Hotels have the privilege to announce that soon will open a new hotel in Cuba, located in the magnificent area of Cayo Santa María.

The Hotel Valentin Perla Blanca***** in Cayo Santa Maria will be an Adults Only resort (Ages 18 +) with more than half a mile white sand beach, located in Playa Laguna III

The property is designed in the Caribbean modern style. This luxurious resort is located 90 minutes from Santa Clara International Airport.

Challenge Mallorca-Paguera 2014

Challenge Mallorca-Paguera 2014

Classical Music Festival
