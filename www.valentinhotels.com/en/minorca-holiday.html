Minorca Surroundings

The beach of Cala’n Bosch is situated at 250 m distance to the Hotel and it has crystal clear water and white sand. At this barrier free beach you can rent sun lounges and umbrellas. You can also enjoy one of the water sport activities offered. The sports harbour with 270 moorings is located at approx. 100 m distance to the hotel. The harbour has been awarded on various occasions the blue flag for fulfilling highest quality standards.

The beach of Son Xoriguer is located at approx. 500 m to the hotel. This beach has a little slope, tranquil water and its opening towards the ocean has a very particular form. On the left side it is very wide and advancing towards the right it gets rather small. The little rocks on the right hand side emerging from the water give the beach an even more picturesque touch. At the local beach bar you can rent umbrellas, lounges and paddleboats.

The old and former capital of the island, Ciutadella, is situated just 8 km from the hotel and surrounded by an old and mistress city wall. In Ciutadella you can go for a stroll through its peculiar and narrow streets which are still hosting the unique charm of the different cultures which have been occupying the city. Closer attention deserves the beautiful cathedral, the different churches and palaces and the typical picturesque harbour.

The city which is known for its cultural and archaeological variety revives every year and old tradition dated back to the 14th century where the patron of the city – San Joan – is dedicated a special celebration which maintains its medieval roots.

To sum it up: The visitor of Ciutadella can enjoy a landscape with crystal clear water, bays and a great number of archaeological treasures, an open air museum.... a true gift for eyes and senses.
