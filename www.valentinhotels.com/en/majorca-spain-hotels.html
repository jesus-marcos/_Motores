Hotel in Majorca

Leave the airport on the Pm-19 and take direction Ma-20. Change at the Ma-20 towards Andratx, Port d´Alcúdia. Once there, change from the Ma-20 on to Ma-13 direction Inca. Take the exit direction Sa Pobla and follow the Ma-3420. There you have to keep on driving towards Muro. Once you get to Muro take left at the first roundabout towards Ma-3431. Stay at the Ma-3431 until you get to Ca´n Picafort.

There you pass the first roundabout and the entrance of Ca´n Picafort. At the second roundabout you follow the indication towards Port d´Alcúdia onto the Ma-12. Once there and after approx. 400 m turn left into the complex Valentín Blue Bay.
