Activities in Minorca

Valentín Star Hotel ****

In collaboration with Menorquad (info@menorquad.com) we offer activities to get to know the island from another perspective.

Tour in Quad or Buggy:
Guided buggy o quad tour at the island, the offer includes assurances, licence number and a bilingual monitor. One vehicle can carry 2 persons. We will pass bays, picturesque places and there is even time to have a bath.
Shuttle to Cala en Blanes not included.
The tours are offered in the morning and the afternoon (3 hours).
Prize per vehicle and tour 70€

Horse Rides:
The guided ride passes by the north part of the island, you can choose from a huge variety of horses, the offer includes an experienced guide, insurance and all the necessary material.
Shuttle to Cala en Blanes not included.
Duration 2 hours
Prize per person 40€

Paintball:
This activity consists in two teams that play against each other, using guns with colour cartridges. You can choose between different games in an adequate place in Ciutadella. Includes insurance, 400 cartridges and all the necessary material.
Shuttle to Cala en Blanes not included.
Duration 2 hours
Prize per person 30€

To book the offers, simply book the hotel and in the next step you can select them.
More information: s.vogt@valentinhotels.com o t.vich@valentinhotels.com

Diving Baptism

Valentín Sancti Petri Spa & Convention Centre ****


We offer a diving baptism in collaboration with the Centro de Buceo Ocean Addicts near Tarifa.

It is a unique experience to submerge in crystal clear water and enjoy seeing fishes, plants and rock formations.

This excursion for 120€ for 2 persons is for beginners and includes an experienced monitor, insurances, all the necessary material and the shuttle from the hotel. You also will be given theoretical information.
The time in the water is about 1 hour.
More information: info@oceanaddicts.es

To book the excursion, simply book the hotel and in the next step you can select it.

Once the booking is confirmed, we will send the exact dates and information.
For more information contact with: s.vogt@valentinhotels.com or t.vich@valentinhotels.com

Sunset Boat Trip

Valentín Star Hotel ****

Enjoy this fantastic and romantic boat trip and see one of the most beautiful sunsets of the Mediterranean Sea.
The trip starts at Cala Galdana (the transfer to the cove, approx. 20 min away from the hotel, is not included) and drives past some of the most beautiful coves of Menorca.

The tour (3 hours) for 50€ p.p. includes two stops to take a bath, cocktail and aperitif with typical products of the island made by the captain Nano and the trip to Cabo Artrutx where you can enjoy the sunset at sea with a glass of sparkling water. (Flyer)

To book the boat trip, reserve first your room, then in the next step you can choose the day for the trip.

More information: info@menorcaenbarco.com, www.menorcaenbarco.com

For journeys from 01/05/2012 - 31/10/2012
(VAT included, minimum stay from 4 nights)
The prize of the trip does not include the accommodation
