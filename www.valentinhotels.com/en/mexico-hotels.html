Hotels in Mexico

The world famous area of Cancún is one of the luxury class tourist centres of unmatched beauty. The surroundings reveal an exciting marina with turquoise, crystal clear water and palm trees. The lagoon of Nichupté, which is divided by an emerging coral reef from the open sea, captivates with its closeness to the tropical forest and its fascinating views. It is one of most visited national parks of Quitana Roo. The Hotels of Cancún with a characteristically daring architecture, modern and functional, offer all visitors a wide range of high quality service, such as water sport activities and other kind of entertainment. The white sand of all beaches with its turquoise and warm water stands out and invites you to enjoy a truly unique place. Only at a few meters depths growing corals are the entrance to a fascinating under water world. The most visited beaches are Las Perlas, Langosta, Linda, Chacmool and Gaviota Azul.

From Cancún one can visit Chichen Itza, the old capital of the Mayas and one of the symbols of Mexico, Tulum, the ruins of a mayan city at the coast, and other historic Mayan sites such as Xel Ha, the nature park Xcaret and the offshore islands of Cancún, Isla Mujeres and Cozumel. Cancún is a tropical paradise rich in flowers and other plants, where you can enjoy a breathtaking landscape, practice water sport or simply take a sun bath while watching the natives fishing. The beaches are the ideal place to relax by just listening the sound of the waves. Surrounded by the Caribbean Sea Cancuns offers vacation in a unique area where you can discover always something new.

Enthusiast of water sports can find ideal condition for any kind of activities: snorkling, fishing, deep see fishing, diving and excursions by boot starting in Cancún. In addition one can practice waverunner, parachuting and many other things. The eco turism also found its place in Cancún. The closely situated nature park of Xcaret is an ideal starting point in a unique surrounding for any kind of activities related with nature.

Mexico offers a typical native cousine – 4 meals a day. It starts with a breakfast containing eggs mexican style, eggs with sausages, beefsteaks, grilled meat in all kind of variation acompanied by fresh juices and fruits. Lunch is situated between 13.00 and 16.00 where visitors can taste Tamales, stuffed pasta with maiz and cheese, Atole, a drink made of milk and corn flour with different tastes, or finally a stew with tortillas and bread. The starter normaly consists of a soup with rice. For dessert there are many popular mexican sweets to choose from. For the afternoon snack there are once again sweets: chocolate „a la española“ or Champurrado, a warm milk shake. With it they serve bread and the typical Antojitos such as Enchiladas, Pambazos, toasted openfaced sandwiches and tamales (stuffed pastry also served for breakfast), Quesadillas, Tacos and other things. Dinner is usually taken between 20.00 and 21.00. It contains of only one meal, for example tortitas, memelas, Tortillas made of corn flour, cheese and sausages, acomponied by hot chocolate and a piece of sweet bread.
