Rooms in Majorca

In the main building there are 236 rooms, 28 of them with connecting door and in an annexe 36 apartments.

The hotel rooms also have a fully equipped bath room.

In addition the apartments and the hotel rooms have the following amenities:

  • Direct dial telephone
  • Satellite TV
  • Air condition / heating
  • Safety deposit box with charge
